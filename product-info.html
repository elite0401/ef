Overview

ENVIROMOL TM Monolaurate (ML) Series products are produced by the monoester reaction of lauric acid and polyethylene glycol to create hydrophile lipophile surface active properties.
The performance characteristics of the ENVIROMOL™ ML Series materials are determined by the number of alkyl chains, alkyl chain lengths, and degree of polymerization used in the reaction process. ENVIROMOL™ ML Series materials are used as emulsifiers, stabilizers, solubilizers, pearlizers, and viscosity control agents.
