About Us

Environmental Fluids is lead by experts in the field with over a century of combined experience in designing specialty materials for a multitude of industries and applications. We encompass a turn-key approach unlike any other in the chemical business by providing research, production, manufacturing, and distribution services to clients in more than 20 countries.

Turn-key Research

Environmental Fluids is a turn-key chemical research, scale-up, and production company specializing in the synthesis of renewable and bio-derived materials.

Our Mission

Our company’s mission is to improve the lives of others through the research and design of next generation chemical products.

Established in 2005

This mission began back in 2005 as a small group of engineers and chemists set out to improve and enhance the scale-up of surfactant, polymers, and various specialty chemicals.

150+ Engineered Products

The team of scientists have engineered more than 150+ multi-functional materials to date for industries as diverse as paints & coatings to food packaging materials.

Research & Development

Dedicated researchers work with your company to understand the details of your application and how Environmental Fluids can enhance product properties and performance.

Scale-up expert’s transition concept to reality; ensuring all development project parameters are met during the transition from pilot to full scale production.

Production & Processing

Optimized production processes allow for flexible batch sizes and improved product quality. All processes are designed to meet strict regulatory standards including USP-NF, EPA, and FDA monograms.

High throughput flaking, milling, and pulverizing processes transform our high molecular weight materials into unique particle sizes for improved handling of solid state materials.
