Investing in developing innovative technologies

Environmental Fluids' products are designed for the needs of industrial applications as diverse as cleaners and detergents, paints & coatings, hydraulic fluids, lubricants, water treatment, oil field, pulp & paper products, metal working, electronics, textiles, mining, construction, plastics, printing & inks, and rubber.

Established Industry

Environmental Fluids is a privately held company founded by two partners in 2005 as a cGMP/FDA analytical, research, and scale-up laboratory

Bio Based Products

For over a decade, Environmental Fluids has pursued the development of renewable and bio-derived chemical specialties for the industrial, chemical, personal care, and pharmaceutical industries.

Leading Technology

Over the years, our company has worked with leaders in formulation and material science to design specialty products to improve upon preceding generation work.

Comprehensive Solutions

Over the last decade, Environmental Fluids have expanded capabilities passed that of solely scale-up technologies and into a comprehensive solutions provider of research, engineering, production, and distribution services.

Customer Support

Along this journey, we discovered the need to help our clients find bio-derived alternatives to legacy chemistries still present in the marketplace.

150+ Multifunctional Products

Over the last 12 years these efforts have resulted in a product line of 150+ multi-functional surfactants, polymers, and specialty chemicals.

Research

Our dedicated chemists work with your company to research and uncover alternative materials for your project no matter what development stage your company is in.

Here at Environmental Fluids we specialize in the design of renewable and bio-based chemistries for all forms of polymers, surfactants, and specialty chemicals.

Leveraging the latest in technological advancements, our team of researchers have the tools to model and execute novel molecular enhancements in our research laboratory.

Engineering

From concept to industrial scale production, our dedicated chemists work with your team to make advancements a reality.

Our cGMP and FDA certified laboratory is prepared to take on the most challenging and sensitive projects found in the industrial, chemical, personal care, and pharmaceutical industries.

With over a decade of experience in engineering products, we are certain our team of chemists and engineers will provide the expertise needed to solve your company’s technical challenges.

Production

Unlike industry standards, we don’t assess your opportunity based on conventional production rates and minimum volumes.

From pilot scale to full scale production runs, Environmental Fluids is the turn-key development company for your project.

Environmental Fluids production flexibility gives our customers options when it comes to volume requirements.

Distribution

Unlike conventional chemical manufacturers, Environmental Fluids does not stop at distribution.

We understand the need of supply and customer service to clients of all sizes and geographies.

Here at Environmental Fluids, our supply chain and customer service professionals work with your company to ensure product is not just delivered on-time but that we have it available for you when the time is right.
