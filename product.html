Surfactants

Environmental Fluids specializes in the design and production of renewable and bio-based multi-functional surfactants. Our line of surface chemistries are comprehensive, as it encompasses nonionic, cationic, anionic, and amphoteric specialties. Whether you are interested in a legacy chemistry or a custom engineered renewable, Environmental Fluids has the resources and experience to find the ideal solution for you.

View More

Polymers

We are a leader in the production of multifunctional polymer specialties. Our line of polymers are designed around bio-based alternatives, which enhance and reduce the environmental impact of polyurethane, elastomer, and resin formulations. With disciplines in coating rheology, adhesion strength, and thermal stability; Environmental Fluids is the polymer performance specialist for your distinct applications.
